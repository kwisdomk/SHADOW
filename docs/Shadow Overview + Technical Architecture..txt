Protective by necessity.The Problem Shadow SolvesKenya’s mobile-first ecosystem creates enormous convenience — but also exposes millions of users to:Scam SMS Mobile money fraud Fake payment confirmations Safaricom / KRA impersonation SIM swap social engineering Fraudulent links Spam campaigns Emerging local-language scams Most current systems are:Reactive, generic, and easy to bypass.They often:Miss Kenyan linguistic nuance (Swahili, Sheng, code-switching) Overlook local fraud tactics Depend too much on user awareness Fail to evolve fast enough What Shadow IsShadow is not:A chatbot A visible antivirus dashboard A spam folder clone A one-time fraud detector Shadow is:A silent adaptive security layerIt functions more like:A digital immune system for mobile users.Shadow continuously:1.
